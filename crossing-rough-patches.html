Crossing Rough Patches #1

All the wrong escapes and what you should stick to instead

background goes here

Watching reels and shorts

Postponing something a lot because it is new to you and hence you are not getting started

Creating content and hopelessly hoping for likes and views

Trying to learn the enticing tools from docker to typescript and what not

What you can do instead:

  • take shower
  • create but don't wait for likes and views instead get feedback from fellow programmers and improve
  • as a creative outlet think of how you can do things differently in programming domain itself
  • Break the task down, Have "making it easy" sessions to complete higher level difficulty parts
  • work on a project that is close to you and serves you in your spare time
  • grind through the simpler versions first, it will give you confidence in using the tools when working with more complex versions
